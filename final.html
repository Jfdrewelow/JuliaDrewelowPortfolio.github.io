Final Project Content

Description

For my Final Project I used scratch to create a game called Hungry Squirrel (PT 2).

How the game works

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the squirrel back and forth on the screen. Your goal is to catch the muffins, and the powerups, but avoid the rabid squirrels. There are 2 levels and if you loose you will upset the squirrel.
